My hourly rate is $50. However, I am flexible and open to discussing a flat rate for larger projects or ongoing work.

I typically charge based on the scope and complexity of the project. For smaller tasks, I may use an hourly rate, while larger projects often have a fixed price. I'm open to discussing the best approach that fits your needs.
